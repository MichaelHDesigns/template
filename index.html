About MSM Moore Foundation

A world in which every child attains the right to survival, protection, development and participation and to inspire breakthroughs in the way the world treats children and to achieve immediate and lasting change in their lives.

Land for The Homeless

Land for the Homeless is a New Jersey based 501(c)3 public charity, founded at the start of 2015 by Kyler Dineen, which is working to construct a supportive tiny home community on a plot of land it owns in Central NJ for the homeless to be able to live upon legally and in peace, without fear of unwarranted eviction or harassment.

The Salvation Army of Athens, Greece

The Salvation Army is an international Christian movement that operates in 124 countries worldwide. The Salvation Army aims to spread the Christian message of God's love to all, as well as extend humanitarian aid, regardless of politics, religious beliefs or race.

Grassroots Crisis Intervention

Grassroots is a multi-service crisis intervention center providing 24-hour crisis intervention services, emergency and transitional shelter, and community education.

SGQ Urban

Started in 2012, we are a philanthropic initiative that has been setup up to address various objectives with a special focus on developing countries and impoverished nations.
